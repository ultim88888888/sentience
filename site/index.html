State of Crypto:
Doppelganger Edition

a16z crypto's edge is largely internal knowledge + intelligence — so I tried to simulate it. I scraped the team's research, media, and X activity, built time-gated digital doppelgangers of each investor, extracted a market signal, and turned it into a trading strategy. Everything below is point-in-time and lookahead-firewalled.

Stage 2

Doppelganger Creation

Ten a16z crypto investors & researchers, reconstructed from their corpus — rebuilt monthly with only the data available at each point in time.

Each doppelganger = a "soul" (how they reason, distilled from their full corpus) + a time-gated memory feed of what they had actually said by date T.

Stage 3

Extracting Signal

Five ways to turn the doppelgangers into one house market view. Each outputs an outlook — excited / concerned sectors & tokens, conviction, horizon, risk bias. Click any to expand.

Signal strength by approach — cross-sectional information coefficient (t-stat)

Outlook schema: bullish/bearish sectors & tokens · conviction · horizon · reasoning · provenance. An amnesiac Opus pass audited every output for information bleed, taxonomy, and lookahead.

Stage 4

Strategy Creation

The winning signal (council consensus, no digest), traded as a sector rotation. Choose a window to see how it compounds.

Rule: each month, long the top-3 council-bullish sectors that are outperforming BTC (relative-momentum gate) · equal-weight liquid baskets · hold BTC when none qualify · beta-neutralize in quant-defined bear regimes. Quarterly signal refresh, monthly timing.

Scale

$10mm notional, monthly rebalance. Simulations are net of funding & fees, with OI-based liquidity constraints. Optimization via split + walk-forward validation. Best alpha t-stat ≈ 1.9 (n=53) — promising, not formally significant; past performance ≠ future results.

Built end-to-end with Opus 4.8 (high effort), unique agent archetypes by task.
