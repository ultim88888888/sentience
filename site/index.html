Doppelganger Edition

a16z crypto's edge is largely internal knowledge + intelligence — so I tried to simulate it. I scraped the team's research, media, and X activity, built time-gated digital doppelgangers of each investor, extracted a market signal, and turned it into a trading strategy. Everything below is point-in-time, lookahead-firewalled, and built with Opus 4.8.

Stage 3

The Strategy

The winning signal (council consensus, no digest), traded as a sector rotation. Pick a hypothetical deploy date and scale to see how it compounds.

Rule: each month, long the top-3 council-bullish sectors that are outperforming BTC (relative-momentum gate) · equal-weight liquid baskets · hold BTC when none qualify · beta-neutralize in quant-defined bear regimes. Quarterly signal refresh, monthly timing.

Built end-to-end with Opus 4.8 (high effort), unique agent archetypes by task. Simulations net of funding & fees, with OI-based liquidity constraints. Optimization via split + walk-forward validation. Honest caveat: best alpha t-stat ≈1.9 (n=53) — promising, not formally significant. Past performance ≠ future results.
