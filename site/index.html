Stage 2

Doppelganger Creation

Ten a16z crypto investors & researchers, reconstructed from their corpus — rebuilt monthly with only the data available at each point in time. Click any to see their doppelganger profile.

Each doppelganger = a "soul" (how they reason, distilled from their full corpus) + a time-gated memory feed of what they had actually said by date T. A hard ≤T firewall + an amnesiac Opus audit gate ensure no doppelganger ever sees information dated after the evaluation point.
